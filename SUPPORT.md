## Asking for Help

When asking for help, include:

- the document or domain you are working on;
- the exact question or ambiguity;
- links or paths to the relevant files;
- whether the issue is conceptual, structural, data-related, or script-related.

## Medical and Safety Boundary

This repository does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, personal health recommendations, neural intervention advice, or memory manipulation instructions.
