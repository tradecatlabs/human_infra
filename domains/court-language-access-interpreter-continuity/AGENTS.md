## Maintenance Rules

- Do not provide translation, interpretation, legal advice, or local procedure instructions.
- Keep discussion anchored to official language-access policy, court system design, and access barriers.
- Treat language, immigration, nationality, and ethnicity as sensitive context; do not collect or infer them.
- Distinguish qualified interpretation from informal family, child, machine, or ad hoc interpretation.
