## Maintenance Rules

- Discuss filing systems only as access infrastructure, not as instructions for live litigation.
- Never generate live-case filing packets, legal forms, signatures, statements of fact, or service instructions.
- Preserve distinction between federal, state, local, administrative, and tribunal-specific procedures.
- Include paper, in-person, mail, and assisted alternatives when discussing digital filing.
