## Boundaries

- Upstream: `domains/long-term-digital-preservation-format-migration/`, `domains/persistent-identifier-doi-citation-metadata-continuity/`.
- Downstream: source cards, Web paper snapshots, evidence audits and reproducibility packages.
- Do not archive private, copyrighted, restricted, hazardous or confidential materials without permission and governance.

## Maintenance

Use LOCKSS, CLOCKSS, Zenodo, Software Heritage and Internet Archive sources. Separate preservation from permission, validity and endorsement.
