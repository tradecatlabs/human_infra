This directory owns research on court accessibility and disability accommodation as access infrastructure.

## Maintenance Rules

- Do not give legal advice, medical advice, request drafting, evidence strategy, or disability eligibility conclusions.
- Keep court accessibility separate from general building accessibility, healthcare accessibility, and workplace accommodation.
- Treat disability, health, neurodivergence, and communication needs as sensitive data.
- Preserve both physical and digital accessibility boundaries.
