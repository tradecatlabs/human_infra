## Boundaries

- Upstream: `domains/c4-conversion-channel/research-infrastructure-open-science-translation/`.
- Downstream: research data repositories, research software archiving and trustworthy scientific communication.
- Do not provide metric manipulation, citation spam, publisher evasion or fake identifier guidance.

## Maintenance

Use DOI Foundation, Crossref and DataCite as primary references. Separate resolvability from validity: a stable identifier is not proof that a claim is true.
