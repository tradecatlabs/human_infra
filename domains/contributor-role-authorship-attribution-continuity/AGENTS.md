## Boundaries

- Upstream: `domains/scholarly-identity-orcid-affiliation-continuity/`, `domains/trustworthy-scientific-communication-peer-review/`.

## Maintenance

Use CRediT/NISO, ICMJE, ORCID and Crossref as anchors. Keep role attribution distinct from legal ownership and employment obligations.
