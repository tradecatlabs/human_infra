## Maintenance Rules

- Do not provide legal advice, complaint drafting, evidence strategy, settlement negotiation, collection advice, or jurisdiction analysis.
- Keep small claims distinct from consumer service complaints, mediation, debt collection, landlord-tenant eviction, and formal civil litigation.
- Discuss dispute systems through access, cost, delay, fairness, and recovery continuity.
- Avoid publishing party names, addresses, case facts, or reputation-damaging allegations.
