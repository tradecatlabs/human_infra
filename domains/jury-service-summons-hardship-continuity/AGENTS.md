## Maintenance Rules

- Do not advise on summons response, excuses, deferrals, voir dire, jury nullification, or evasion.
- Treat juror identity, addresses, employment, disability, health, hardship, and case information as sensitive.
- Separate jury service from voting, court hearings, employment law, and disability accommodation while preserving interfaces.
- Keep all examples non-jurisdiction-specific unless quoting official public materials.
