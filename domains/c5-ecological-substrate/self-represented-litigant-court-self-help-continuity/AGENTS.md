## Maintenance Rules

- Keep content at public-information and research-boundary level.
- Do not provide legal advice, case strategy, form completion, deadline calculation, or local court instructions.
- Separate neutral court information, legal aid referral, and legal representation.
- Preserve privacy and avoid collecting case numbers, addresses, parties, immigration status, disability status, or sensitive family facts.
