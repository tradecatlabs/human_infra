# Conflict of Interest, Funding and Disclosure Continuity

## Scope

This domain studies conflict-of-interest disclosure, funding statements, sponsor roles, competing interests, transparency declarations and disclosure updates across publications, datasets, code and research communication.

Human Infra treats disclosure continuity as an evidence-quality condition: a claim cannot be evaluated well if the incentives, sponsors and competing interests around it disappear.

## Human Infra Chain

```text
research claim enters public reasoning
  -> claim may be shaped by funding, employment, ownership, consulting or sponsor influence
  -> disclosure records expose relevant incentive context
  -> readers can evaluate bias risk, independence and interpretation limits
  -> evidence can be weighted without relying only on narrative confidence
  -> long-term model governance becomes more robust
```

## Non-Goals

- No legal, employment, grant compliance or publication-submission advice.
- No accusation of misconduct based solely on funding or relationships.
- No private financial, employment, grant, review or editorial data collection.
- No guidance for hiding, minimizing or laundering conflicts.
