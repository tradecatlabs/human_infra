# Evidence Certainty GRADE Domain

## Purpose

This directory covers GRADE-style certainty judgments, summary-of-findings tables and evidence strength boundaries.

## Files

```text
evidence-certainty-grade-summary-findings-continuity/
├── AGENTS.md
└── README.md
```

## Boundaries

- Upstream: `domains/risk-of-bias-critical-appraisal-continuity/`, `domains/meta-analysis-effect-size-heterogeneity-continuity/`.
- Downstream: evidence-to-decision frameworks, guideline interpretation and Human Infra model claim strength.
- Do not provide clinical recommendations, coverage decisions, certainty inflation, diagnosis, therapy ranking or individual risk prediction.

## Maintenance

Use GRADE Handbook, GRADE Working Group, GRADEpro and Cochrane materials as anchors. Certainty labels must retain downgrade reasons.
