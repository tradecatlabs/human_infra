# 0029. Add Twenty-Fifth-Wave Civilizational Continuity And Collective Security Domains

Date: 2026-06-30

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

Human Infra 已经覆盖个人身体、认知、照护、公共服务、外部化主体资料和高风险技术底座。但有效永生 / 主体持续性最大化还依赖更上游的集体条件：战争不能直接清零平民未来，社会冲突不能持续升级，公共机构不能在危机中停止基本职能，通信网络不能让主体失联，宏观经济不能把基本资源变得不可负担，公共权力不能被腐败俘获。

这些条件不能只归入 `risk-engineering/`、`governance-rights/`、`public-service-design-accessibility/`、`emergency-preparedness-response/` 或 `information-integrity-trust/`。它们各自拥有独立机制、证据体系和失败模式：武装冲突、社会撕裂、政府中断、通信断线、宏观失稳和腐败会从集体层面同时破坏多个个人级持续性条件。

## Decision

Add six formal domains:

- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/armed-conflict-civilian-protection-ihl/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/peacebuilding-conflict-prevention-social-cohesion/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/continuity-of-government-public-administration-resilience/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/telecommunications-network-resilience-continuity/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/macroeconomic-monetary-fiscal-stability/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/anti-corruption-public-integrity-accountability/`

Update root navigation, docs navigation, reference maps, radar, ethics boundaries, changelog and repository checks so the new domains are discoverable and validated.

## Consequences

- Civilizational continuity, collective security and macro-institutional stability become first-class Human Infra layers.
- Human Infra can now describe how armed conflict, social fragmentation, government discontinuity, communication failure, macroeconomic instability and corruption constrain subject continuity before individual-level interventions even matter.
- Safety boundaries must explicitly prohibit tactical conflict guidance, political manipulation, sabotage, fraud, corruption facilitation, sanctions evasion, market manipulation and legal/financial/political case advice.
- Future evidence work can organize source cards around IHL, peacebuilding, continuity of government, emergency communications, macroeconomic stability and public integrity.

## Non-Goals

- No military, tactical, weapons, target selection, intelligence, evasion, sabotage or paramilitary guidance.
- No political campaign, protest organization, repression, counter-repression, propaganda, hate, harassment or manipulation advice.
- No intrusion, communication disruption, network exploitation, fraud, corruption facilitation, money laundering, market manipulation, tax evasion or sanctions evasion guidance.
- No legal, financial, tax, investment, welfare, procurement, administrative, political or diplomatic advice for specific cases.
- No claim that institutional stability alone is sufficient for effective immortality; it is an upstream boundary condition.
