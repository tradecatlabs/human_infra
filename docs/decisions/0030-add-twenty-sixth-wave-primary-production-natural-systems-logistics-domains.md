# 0030. Add Twenty-Sixth-Wave Primary Production Natural Systems And Logistics Domains

Date: 2026-06-30

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

Human Infra 已经覆盖个人身体、认知、医疗服务、外部化主体连续性和文明连续性条件。但有效永生 / 主体持续性最大化仍依赖更上游的初级生产、自然系统和物理流通底座：食物必须被生产出来，土壤和土地必须维持功能，水资源必须可观测和调度，生态系统必须继续提供服务，野火不能反复摧毁居住与空气安全，关键物资必须通过物流和冷链真实到达人。

这些条件不能只归入 `food-security-nutrition-access/`、`planetary-health-environment/`、`weather-climate-observation-forecasting/`、`water-sanitation-hygiene-continuity/`、`supply-chain-continuity/` 或 `risk-engineering/`。它们各自拥有独立机制、证据体系和失败模式：农业生产、土壤土地、水文、生态系统服务、野火和物流节点会在资源进入个人生活之前决定主体持续性的基础可行性。

## Decision

Add six formal domains:

- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/agricultural-production-food-system-resilience/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/soil-health-land-degradation-drought-resilience/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/water-resources-hydrology-flood-drought-management/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/biodiversity-ecosystem-services-resilience/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/wildfire-landscape-fire-resilience/`
- `domains/c5-ecological-substrate/freight-logistics-port-cold-chain-continuity/`

Update root navigation, docs navigation, reference maps, radar, ethics boundaries, changelog and repository checks so the new domains are discoverable and validated.

## Consequences

- Primary production, natural capital, hydrology, ecosystem services, wildfire resilience and physical logistics become first-class Human Infra layers.
- Human Infra can now describe how food production, soil degradation, water scarcity, biodiversity loss, wildfire and freight/cold-chain failure constrain subject continuity before individual-level choices or medical interventions matter.
- Safety boundaries must explicitly prohibit operational farming/chemical advice, land/commodity speculation, real-time disaster action, logistics exploitation, customs/sanctions evasion, sabotage and sensitive infrastructure targeting.
- Future evidence work can organize source cards around FAO, UNCCD, WMO, IPBES, CBD, UNDRR, USFA/NIFC, World Bank LPI and UNCTAD logistics sources.

## Non-Goals

- No farming, livestock, pesticide, fertilizer, veterinary, irrigation, land transaction or commodity speculation advice.
- No real-time flood, drought, wildfire, evacuation, firefighting, logistics routing or engineering operation guidance.
- No customs evasion, sanctions evasion, smuggling, illegal transport, sabotage, intrusion, sensitive infrastructure exploitation or supply disruption guidance.
- No ecosystem service valuation as investment, land acquisition, carbon market, subsidy, project approval or legal conclusion.
- No claim that natural-system resilience alone is sufficient for effective immortality; it is an upstream boundary condition.
