# Add One-Hundred-Thirty-First Wave Immune Aging Systemic Signal Domains

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

`immune-maintenance/` gives Human Infra a broad immune-system maintenance domain, and `cellular-senescence-clearance/`, `biological-age-clocks-biomarker-validation/`, `blood-oxygen-hemostasis/`, and `cancer-control/` already cover adjacent parts of the aging system.

That still leaves several high-leverage immune-aging and systemic-signal mechanisms under-modeled as independent research objects. Thymic involution, immune repertoire aging, clonal hematopoiesis, inflammaging, plasma proteome aging signals, and extracellular vesicle / SASP communication each has its own source base, variable set, measurement hazards, misuse risks, and relation to effective immortality.

These domains matter because long-lived subjects must keep adapting to new antigens, avoid malignant or inflammatory clonal drift, maintain low-risk systemic communication, and distinguish plausible biomarkers from unproven anti-aging interventions.

## Decision

Add six immune-aging and systemic rejuvenation-signal continuity domains:

- `domains/thymic-involution-naive-t-cell-output-continuity/`
- `domains/immune-repertoire-vaccine-response-aging-continuity/`
- `domains/clonal-hematopoiesis-chip-inflammatory-risk-continuity/`
- `domains/inflammaging-chronic-low-grade-inflammation-continuity/`
- `domains/plasma-proteome-systemic-aging-signal-continuity/`
- `domains/extracellular-vesicle-sasp-intercellular-aging-continuity/`

Each domain must keep `README.md` and `AGENTS.md` as its local truth source. Each domain is a research, source-card, model-variable, visualization, and evidence-boundary object rather than an individual medical, testing, product, or therapy recommendation surface.

## Consequences

- Immune aging is no longer compressed into a single general immune-maintenance bucket.
- The project can separately model thymic output, immune repertoire diversity, hematopoietic clonal drift, chronic inflammatory state, circulating proteomic signals, and EV/SASP intercellular communication.
- The new domains create clearer bridges between longevity biology, cancer control, vaccine response, biomarker validation, blood biology, nanomedicine, and cellular senescence.
- Safety boundaries must remain strict because these topics are easily misread as advice for immune enhancement, young plasma, exosome products, anti-inflammatory self-treatment, or genetic test interpretation.

## Non-Goals

- Do not provide thymic regeneration, immune enhancement, stem-cell, hormone, drug, supplement, or experimental protocol advice.
- Do not provide vaccine scheduling, booster selection, antibody threshold interpretation, infection-risk personalization, or clinical immunology guidance.
- Do not interpret individual CHIP, genetic sequencing, blood count, mutation, cancer-risk, cardiovascular-risk, or hematology-monitoring results.
- Do not recommend anti-inflammatory drugs, supplements, diets, procedures, or biomarker-driven treatment plans.
- Do not recommend young plasma, plasma exchange, transfusion, blood products, commercial plasma services, or anti-aging blood interventions.
- Do not recommend exosome products, injections, cell culture, extraction, EV testing, or regenerative-medicine commercial offerings.
